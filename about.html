Ando Marin

Ando waddddds born in Colombia and currently lives in Toronto, Canada, where he enjoys taking photos of trees in urban environments. In his work he explores the city as an extension of the mind of its observer but also its feedback effect: how the cities we live in shape our minds and our maps of meaning. He uses a combination of medium format film, 35mm film and digital to capture his images.
